How To Make a Batik Headband

Our group chose a batik headband for our product because batik is a beautiful motif and a symbol of our culture passed down from generation to generation. It’s also a cute accessory that makes your hair more aesthetically pleasing and neat.

Tools and Materials

Tools:

  • Fabric scissor
  • Meter Ruler
  • Pencil
  • Sewing machine
  • Sewing needles
  • Needle pins
  • Iron
  • Safety pins

Materials:

  • Batik fabric
  • Elastic band (20cm x 2cm)
  • Sewing thread
  • Baking paper

Instructions

1. First, the pattern for the headband is drawn using a pencil and meter ruler on baking paper as the template. These are the patterns:
2. After that, the batik fabric is cut with fabric scissors.
3. The batik fabric is then attached to the baking paper using needle pins.
4. Next, cut the batik fabric according to both of the patterns you’ve made.
5. Now we have the big piece and the small one. Fold the big piece (bad side) into two and insert the needle pins.
6. The fabric is sewn into one piece by the sewing machine. Flip it inside out afterward.
7. Do the same steps (4-5) on the smaller piece of fabric.
8. After both fabrics are sewn, the smaller piece of fabric is inserted into the plastic band.
9. The elastic band must be smaller than the piece of fabric.
10. Lastly, the bigger piece of fabric and the smaller piece of fabric are sewn into one using the sewing machine.
Tips:
  • Iron the finished batik headband to make it look neater.
  • Be careful when working with the sewing machine and iron.
  • Make sure your elastic band is not loose and is sewn well.

How To Make a Batik Drawstring Pouch

Before I’m going to show you the tutorial, I’ll tell you why we chose this product to be in our shop. This pouch is very aesthetic and practical. Not only that, but this pouch is also a symbol of our culture. Anyway, here are the items you need to prepare.

Tools and Materials

Tools:

  • Fabric scissor
  • Meter Ruler
  • Pencil
  • Sewing machine
  • Sewing needles
  • Needle pins
  • Iron
  • Safety pins

Materials:

  • Batik fabric
  • Drawstring fabric
  • Sewing thread
  • Baking paper

Instructions

1. First, a pencil is used to draw the pattern on the baking paper. These patterns will be used as a template for the pouch’s size and shape. This is the pattern:
2. Once done, carefully cut them out using fabric scissors before using the needle pins to attach the baking paper to the batik fabric.
3. Next, cut the batik fabric according to the pattern that was created earlier.
4. Then, the batik fabric is folded horizontally.
5. Next, the fabric is sewn into one by the sewing machine. Be sure to leave a small hole (about 1,5 to 2 cm) on each side of the fabric.
6. After that, cut two drawstring fabrics according to your preferred length.
7. To insert the drawstring, use safety pins and attach them at the end of both drawstring fabrics.
8. Then, the hole in the pouch you made earlier is inserted using the drawstring fabric (one on each side).
9. After inserting the drawstring, pull both ends through the holes on both sides before tying the knots to secure it.
10. Finally, the fabric is flipped inside out to reveal the finished pouch.

Now then, your pouch is ready to be used! Keys, lip balm, earbuds, and other small essentials can be stored inside the batik drawstring pouch. This way, you can have immediate access to your belongings in this beautiful bag.

Tips:
  • Iron the finished drawstring pouch to make it look tidier.
  • Be careful when working with the sewing machine and iron.
  • Make sure the thread is strong and small enough to fit into the hole.

How To Make Edible Water Droplets

There are a lot of biotechnology products. One of them is an edible water droplet. Water that has solidified into a bubble-like shape is called an edible water droplet. It doesn't always have to be water. Like us, we use Hydro Coco. We chose this product because it makes one of our drinks, which is coconut water, more appealing and interesting. Not only that, it is also healthy to consume. Here’s how to make it.

Tools and Materials

Tools:

  • 2 basins
  • Tablespoon
  • Measuring glass
  • A large syringe or a spoon that is shaped like a semicircle
  • Blender
  • Bowl of water and ice

Ingridients:

  • 10 grams of calcium chloride
  • 2 grams of sodium alginate
  • 500 ml of Hydro Coco (or any drink you’ll like)
  • 2000 ml of water

Instructions

1. First, 2 grams of sodium alginate and 500 ml of Hydro Coco are mixed using the blender.
2. Make sure it is fully mixed and there’s no more lump left before putting the mixed liquid inside the first basin.
3. Then, wait for 15-20 minutes so the air bubbles inside the mixed fluid escape.
4. While waiting, another basin is being used to pour the 2000 ml of water and the 10 grams of calcium chloride.
5. Next, mix it until it is smooth.
6. After that, a large syringe or a spoon shaped like a semi-circle is used to take The sodium alginate liquid from the first basin.
7. Afterward, slowly dip the sodium alginate liquid into the calcium chloride liquid.
8. Then, the sodium alginate liquid is removed from the calcium chloride liquid once it starts to form a ball. The process for the sodium alginate liquid to form a ball will take about 1 to 2 minutes.
9. A bowl of water and ice is used to put the newly made edible water droplet before consuming or packing it.
10. Lastly, you can put the edible water (Hydro Coco) droplet inside coconut water (optional).

That’s how to make an edible water droplet using Hydro Coco. Enjoy the refreshing and healthy drink.

Tips:
  • Be careful while handling the edible water droplet when making it because it can easily pop.
  • The bigger the sodium alginate you put on the basin (step 6), the bigger the water droplet will be.
