Abstract

On this website, we will explain our final Integrated Learning Project (ILP), where we organized a bazaar and art performance that took place on January 14th and 16th, 2025, at Saint Ursula Jakarta Middle School. results of these will be explained on this website. The theme for this year’s ILP is Nusantara which is adapted to the name Puspanusa for this event. The diversity of Indonesia’s culture is reflected in Puspanusa. As part of the theme, our group decided to choose Java, specifically East Java is chosen as our main theme. With that, we named our stall “Javest,” short for “Java Feast and Festival”. East Java is a beautiful province with a rich culture, natural beauty, and amazing traditions. Through this project, we all learn more about our country’s cultural heritage and use our creativity to combine our cultural heritage with the modern era.

Batik crafts, cute accessories, and delicious and unique food and drinks are featured in our stall. As a token of appreciation, freebies were given out to the customer by us. East Java-like ornaments are used to decorate our booth. Examples are the night delicious flower (bunga sedap malam) and our mascots which are the animals from Indonesia. We learn during the bazaar that opening a stall or shop is harder than it looks. We need to use our creativity, budgeting skills, fast critical thinking, etc. During the bazaar, it was also very crowded, and we needed to think and act fast while also promoting. We didn’t expect most of our items to sell out so fast, while others are hard to sell. Good thing everything sold out in the end.

In addition to the bazaar, there was also an art performance where collaboration with another group was done by us. The art performance we chose is a drama musical about a teenage friendship that has a miscommunication with one another. We chose this topic so the audiences, especially those of the same age as us, can relate to it. The story tells us about the importance of communication between friends and not framing others for amusement. Popular nostalgic Indonesian songs were incorporated into the drama musical, one of which is Laskar Pelangi. Not only that, but also we incorporated dancing choreography, funny, and magical scenes, making the drama more interesting.

Throughout the whole process, there were lots of obstacles like coming up with ideas, communication challenges, time management, figuring out how to make the product and preparing for the drama musical, and many more had to be faced. Different roles and responsibilities were given to the members to ensure a successful and faster outcome. But even with that, it’s still difficult to balance it out. There were also a few conflicts in our group because of different opinions. It was a very stressful and exhausting journey. But despite all of the hardships, valuable lessons were taught, such as teamwork, making decisions, dealing with rejection, being confident, and many more. It also helps to enhance my marketing, sewing, communication, time management, and cooking skills. With that, this Integrated Learning Project overall makes us grow stronger, wiser when doing something, more creative, and more critical.
